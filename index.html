Neroshop is a user-friendly and censorship-resistant distributed P2P marketplace for Monero users.

What makes neroshop different

  1. There are no fees or restrictions.
  2. All data is private and secure.
  3. The payment is with Monero (XMR) a digital currency that is designed to be untraceable and anonymous.
